About

I am a quantitative researcher and a PhD candidate in Theoretical Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. My research explores scattering of massless Dirac fermions in modulated graphene structures and its effect on electron transport. In industry, I have contributed to Model Risk Management, including diagnostics, benchmarking, and sensitivity analysis for Tier‑1 Risk & Capital models under FRTB, and validation of AI/ML models for identity verification.
